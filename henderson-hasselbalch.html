Henderson-Hasselbalch equation

\[pH = pK_{a} + log_{10}\left( \frac{[A^{-}]}{[HA]}\right )\]

The Henderson-Hasselbalch equation is primarily used to estiamte the pH of a buffer solution.
The Conjugate Base [\(A^{-}\)] and Acid [\(HA\)] are measured in Molars \(M\).

How to use: Select which value to solve for, then enter information.
