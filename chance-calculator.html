Chance Calculator

\[1 - (1-P(A))^n\]

The chance calculator solves for the odds of an event occurring atleast once with \(P(A)\) chance after \(n\) attempts.

This can be used to see how lucky/unlucky you might be for an probability based event to occur.
For example: In video games, if an item has a 1% drop chance, and you loot 100 times, you are \(1 - (1-.01)^{100} = ~0.63397 \) (63.397%) probable to get it
